Wordpress

Integrating PushFeedback into your WordPress website can be a straightforward process. This guide will walk you through the necessary steps to successfully incorporate the PushFeedback tool.

Prerequisites​

  • An active WordPress website.
  • Admin access to your WordPress dashboard.
  • Your PushFeedback project ID.

1. Log in to Your WordPress Dashboard​

Access your WordPress dashboard typically by navigating to:


Replace yourwebsite.com with your website's actual domain.

2. Navigate to Appearance > Theme Editor​

In the WordPress dashboard:

  1. Hover over Appearance on the left sidebar.
  2. Click on Theme Editor.

3. Edit the Header or Footer​

Depending on where you want the PushFeedback scripts to load:

  1. On the right side, locate the Theme Header (header.php) or Theme Footer (footer.php) file.
  2. Click on the respective file to edit.

4. Add the PushFeedback Script​

In the header.php (before the closing </head> tag) or footer.php (before the closing </body> tag) file, paste the following:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/pushfeedback/dist/pushfeedback/pushfeedback.css">
<script type="module" src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/pushfeedback/dist/pushfeedback/pushfeedback.esm.js"></script>

7. Verify Installation​

Visit your website to ensure that the PushFeedback button displays and functions correctly.

Final Thoughts​
