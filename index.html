Quickstart

Welcome to PushFeedback docs! This guide will help you set up and start using the PushFeedback widget in no time.

1. Create your PushFeedback account​

Before you can start receiving feedback, you need to create your own PushFeedback account:

  1. Go to app.pushfeedback.com.
  2. Click on the Sign Up button located in the top bar.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account.

Once your account is ready, you can proceed to create your first project.

2. Create a project​

To create a project:

  1. Navigate to the Projects tab located on the top navbar of your PushFeedback dashboard.
  2. Click on the Create project button.
  3. Follow the wizard, filling in any required information about your project.

After completing the setup, you'll be presented with your Project ID. Make sure to note it down as you'll need it in the next step.

3. Add the widget to your site​

To add the PushFeedback widget to your site, follow the steps:

  1. Embed the following snippet into the HTML of your website:
<!-- Load the PushFeedback widget -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/pushfeedback/dist/pushfeedback/pushfeedback.css">
<script type="module" src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/pushfeedback/dist/pushfeedback/pushfeedback.esm.js"></script>
  1. Insert the following HTML element where you want the feedback button to appear.
<feedback-button project="<YOUR_PROJECT_ID>">Send feedback</feedback-button>

Replace <YOUR_PROJECT_ID> with your project's ID from PushFeedback.

4. Customize the widget​

PushFeedback provides various properties to customize the widget to fit your website's aesthetics and functional requirements. For detailed customization options, refer to our Documentation.

Next steps​

You've successfully set up the PushFeedback widget. With the widget installed and active, your users can now submit feedback directly from your site.

As users interact and provide feedback, regularly check your dashboard to gather insights and make improvements.
