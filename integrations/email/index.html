Email

PushFeedback provides a seamless way to gather insights directly from your users. One of the essential features it offers is the ability to forward the feedback you receive directly to specific email addresses. This feature ensures that the right people in your team are notified promptly about the feedback, ensuring faster responses and better user experience.

Configuring Multiple Email Recipients​

You can configure the PushFeedback module to send the collected feedback to up to 3 email addresses. This is especially beneficial for larger teams where feedback might be relevant to multiple departments, such as design, development, and customer service.

Steps to Configure Multiple Email Recipients:​

  1. Access the PushFeedback Dashboard: Log into your PushFeedback account and navigate to the settings section of your project.

  2. Email Configuration: Look for the email configuration section.

  3. Add Email Addresses: Here, you can add up to 3 separate email addresses. Ensure they are separated by commas. For instance: design@example.com,dev@example.com,support@example.com.

  4. Save Changes: Once you've added the desired email addresses, make sure to save your changes.

Note: Ensure that the provided email addresses are valid and have whitelisted or allowed notifications from PushFeedback to prevent them from landing in the spam folder.
