Troubleshooting​

Facing issues with PushFeedback? Here are some common problems and their solutions:

  • Problem with installation: Ensure you've followed our installation guide step-by-step.
  • Feedback not being sent: Make sure the provided email addresses in the configuration are correct and have whitelisted notifications from PushFeedback.
  • Styling issues: Refer to our design customization guide to adjust the appearance of the feedback module.

Still can't resolve your issue? Reach out to us directly!
